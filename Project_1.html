PROJECT 1. EXPLORE WEATHER TRENDS¶

Step 1: Extract the data from the database¶

First, using SQL the name of the cities in my country, Spain, was searched. Barcelona was chosen as the nearest city to my hometown.

Then the weather data from Barcelona was downloaded as a CSV also using SQL.

Finally, the global weather data was downloaded as a CSV.

Step 2: Open up the CSV¶

The CSV was open using Excel. Then text was converted into columns to be able to operate with them. In the data from Barcelona - Spain, there was found also the data from Barcelona - Venezuela and it was deleted. Furthermore, the data of the two CSV was merged into one XLSX.

Step 3: Create a line chart¶

To represent the temeprature data of the world and Barcelona a moving average of 7 years was done. Then the data was imported and opened to check that everything was correct.

Step 4: Observe the chart line¶

Some conclusions about the chart line:

  • Barcelona is a hotter city than the global average, as the temperature is almost the double.
  • The temperature since 1975 has increased in Barcelona and in the world.
  • Since 1850 the global temperature has benn increasing quite constantly, while in Spain it has fluctuated.
  • Before 1850 the temperature fluctuated more in Barcelona and in the world.
